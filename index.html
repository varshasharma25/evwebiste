INTRODUCTION

The Language of Engineering

Engineering Visualization is the language of engineers. It involves the graphical representation of physical objects and systems, allowing for precise communication between designers, architects, and manufacturers.

Engineering curves like Cycloids, Involutes, and Spirals play a crucial role in mechanical and civil engineering, finding applications in gear tooth profiles, bridge arches, and specialized mechanical paths.
